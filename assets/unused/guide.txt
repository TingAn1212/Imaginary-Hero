Welcome to the city of imagination.
In this city, you have to power to do anything,
as long as you imagine hard enough.
Start your adventure here, and imagine as many abilities as you can!

-You have 3 minutes each game

-Some things you can do are: spam buttons, touch stuff, explore, find hints
